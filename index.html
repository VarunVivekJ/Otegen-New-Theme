About Us

OTEGEN GLOBAL INCORPORATION is a Certified Ayurvedic Formulations Manufacturing Company having its Manufacturing Plant at Faridabad in Haryana and Marketing – cum – Registered office at Delhi. The Company has installed high capacity, state – of- the art automatic plant & Machinery at its factory to carry on production of high quality ayurvedic products uninterruptedly. The Company has its own fully equipped R & D Facility and well – qualified professionals to meet any challenge.


OTEGEN are the pioneers in encapsulation of herbal extracts with utmost efficiency and quality control with a view to unravel the mystery of Ayurveda widely from the old Classical from to contemporary/modern style i.e. Capsule from. Our contribution to the modernization of ayurvedic medicine system without disturbing natural effects of the plants and herbs is being appreciated by the Medical profession and Chemists alike.
